Field: Sub Section
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Version: 1.009
Author: Rowan Lewis <rowan@pixelcarnage.com>
Build Date: 29 January 2009
Requirements: Symphony 2


Installation
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1.  Upload the 'subsectionfield' folder in this archive to your Symphony
  'extensions' folder.

2.  Enable it by selecting the "Field: Sub Section", choose Enable from the
  with-selected menu, then click Apply.

4. You can now add the "Sub Section" field to your sections.
